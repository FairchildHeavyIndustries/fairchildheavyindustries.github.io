Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the card style or table color?

Tap the menu icon in the top-right corner to open the settings tray. Card style and table color options are listed there.

What are the 32,000 deals?

The deals are the original Microsoft FreeCell numbered games (1–32,000), fully compatible with the classic Windows version. Every deal is solvable except deal #11982.

How do I jump to a specific deal number?

Open the settings tray and tap "Edit game number" to enter any deal from 1 to 32,000.

How do I undo a move?

Tap any card that has already been moved — the game will step back one move at a time. You can undo as many times as you like.

How do I remove ads?

Tap the menu icon and select "Remove Ads" for a one-time purchase. Ads are removed permanently across reinstalls as long as you use the same App Store account.

My game disappeared after reinstalling. Can I recover it?

In-progress games are stored locally on your device and are not backed up to the cloud. Reinstalling will start a fresh game, but your "Remove Ads" purchase can always be restored.
