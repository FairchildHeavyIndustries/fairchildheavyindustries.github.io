About

I've spent 24 years inside the systems that run the airline industry — the 40-year-old legacy platforms nobody wants to talk about, and the modern Offer/Order infrastructure everyone's racing to build.

At Radixx (Sabre), I led the design and delivery of Offer/Order capabilities on top of a live PSS — interline seat sales, dynamic ancillary pricing, codesharing — while keeping legacy ticketing and DCS requirements intact. Before that, at Amadeus, I built airline reservation solutions and worked directly with carriers to solve hard integration problems.

I'm not here to sell you on OOSD as a traveler revolution. Airlines need to modernize their retailing infrastructure for operational and commercial reasons, and the technical lift is significant. I'm here to help you ship it.

I started coding before I started managing, and I never really stopped caring about the craft of building software. Today I'm back to hands-on technical work — architecting solutions, writing code, and helping teams deliver things that actually work.
