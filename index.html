About

Fairchild Heavy Industries LLC is a technology consulting and software development firm based in Carolina, Puerto Rico. Founded in April 2026 by Alexander Fairchild, the firm builds software, designs websites, develops cross-platform mobile apps, runs digital marketing and lead generation, and advises on technical strategy across industries.

The name is intentionally weighty. We treat what we build as industrial work — engineered, durable, and accountable to the people who depend on it. That applies whether the deliverable is a mobile game, a small-business website, an airline distribution system, or a marketing pipeline.

We are AI-native by default. Agentic AI is part of how we research, design, code, test, and ship — and increasingly, part of what we ship. That makes a small firm move like a much larger one, and lets us take on work across sectors without diluting quality.

The founder, Alexander Fairchild, has 34 years in technology, including senior roles at Sabre and Amadeus leading global engineering and product teams. Today the work spans heavy equipment distribution, consumer apps, and airline technology — wherever good software and clear thinking move the needle.
